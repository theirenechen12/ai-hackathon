The project, EduTrivia, addresses the challenge of making history and science engaging through interactive quizzes, enhanced with a chatbot that allows learners to ask questions and receive instant explanations. It includes five modules—French Revolution, American Revolution, Cal State LA History, Joseon Dynasty, and Isaac Newton & Scientific Revolution—covering key events, figures, and discoveries, with unique questions that mix historical context, math, and physics. Unlike most quizzes, the chatbot component encourages curiosity, supports deeper understanding, and makes learning both personalized and interactive.
